100 days nightmare series, where Avid first starts in a village he named "New Salem". He also named an iron golem Jeremy, who later dies during a blood moon. To protect this town from blood moons Avid agrees to a deal with a powerful guy named Olm, The Old One. He defeats the "Demon of Darkness" and goes to Limbo, where there is no faith of returning. Avid agrees to a deal with Olm to send a piece of his soul out of limbo, into the land of pure void.
